Objective: To democratize digital inclusivity by providing a free, automated diagnostic tool that evaluates Korean websites against KWCAG 2.2 accessibility standards.

Core Value: Developed to bridge the information gap in the Korean IT market, replacing high-cost, opaque consulting with transparent data, real-time visual analytics, and actionable remediation guides.
